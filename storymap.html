StoryMap

Narrative architecture: map, structure, and evolve stories across any medium.

  • Structured thinking, not writing. StoryMap is the layer beneath the writing: a system for developing story logic, character relationships, and narrative coherence so the story grows tall, not wide.
  • Guided clarity. Prompted intake helps creators define what they know, surface what they don't, and keep the vision sharp as complexity grows.
  • Medium-agnostic. Film, television, novels, comics, games: StoryMap works with the structure of story itself, not the format it ships in.
