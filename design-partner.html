Become a Design Partner

World Tree is being built in collaboration with working creators. Design Partners get early access to each tool as it enters beta, starting with StoryMap.

What we're looking for: Storytellers actively working on a project. Writers, filmmakers, game designers, comic creators. People who know what's broken about their current workflow.

What you get: Beta access to each tool before public release. A direct line to shape the direction of development. Monthly check-ins where your feedback directly informs what gets built next.

What we ask: Use the tools on a real project. Share feedback and bug reports as you go. Join a brief monthly video check-in. That's it.

These tools are being built for creators, by a creator. The Design Partner program exists to make sure that's not just a tagline.
