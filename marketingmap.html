MarketingMap

Marketing strategy mapping: align creative intent with audience reach.

MarketingMap is a structured environment for defining audience, positioning, and communication strategy. It maps target audiences, messaging themes, and positioning goals to the project's core identity, applying the same structural philosophy as StoryMap and ProductionMap to audience-facing strategy.

Currently in the planning phase. Development builds on the upstream clarity established by Index and the Map modules.

Creators know their story better than anyone. But translating that understanding into messaging that resonates with an audience, finding the right positioning, the right language, the right channels, is a different discipline entirely. MarketingMap bridges creative vision and market communication.
