MarketingMap

Marketing strategy mapping: align creative intent with audience reach.

MarketingMap is a structured environment for defining audience, positioning, and communication strategy. It forces clarity around how the project will be presented and discovered in the market, mapping target audiences, messaging themes, and positioning goals connected to the project's core identity.

MarketingMap is currently in the planning phase. Its development will build on the upstream clarity established by Index and the Map modules.

Become a Design Partner

Creators know their story better than anyone. But translating that understanding into messaging that resonates with an audience, finding the right positioning, the right language, the right channels, is a different discipline entirely. MarketingMap bridges the gap between creative vision and market communication.

Guided prompts encourage explicit thinking about audience alignment, differentiation, and market context. The system maintains visibility into how messaging and positioning choices align with project identity, applying the same structural philosophy as StoryMap and ProductionMap to audience-facing strategy.
