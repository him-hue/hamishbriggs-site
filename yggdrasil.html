Coherence

The vision

Yggdrasil

More than the sum of its parts: a Story Operating System built on layers of idea infrastructure.

Each World Tree module solves a distinct problem. Index organises. StoryMap structures. ProductionMap plans. MarketingMap positions. DomainDataMine advises. Individually, they sharpen one dimension of a creative project. Together, they become something else entirely: a unified system where every decision reinforces every other.

Yggdrasil is the integration layer that makes that possible. It connects each module so context flows freely between them, giving creators a single, coherent view of their project from idea through execution.

Ideas do not fail because they are small. They fail because they grow so complex that the relationships fracture, the momentum stalls, and the vision drifts. Yggdrasil exists to prevent that. It is the trunk from which everything branches, and the reason the branches hold.

World Tree does not replace the storyteller. It gives humans the infrastructure to transform complex projects into coherent, actionable reality, without surrendering authorship or control.
