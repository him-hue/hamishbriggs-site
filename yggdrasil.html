Yggdrasil

More than the sum of its parts.

Index provides the foundation. StoryMap gives structure. ProductionMap builds momentum. MarketingMap creates reach. All shaped by targeted insight from DomainDataMine. Yggdrasil takes the best of all of them and puts it in one place: a creative operating system built on real-world usage data, where every dimension of your project lives together and the creator stays in command.

  • Everything in one place. Story, production, marketing, research, and project management in a single system built to work together.
  • Single project view. One coherent picture from first idea through public release. No switching tools, no lost context.
  • Built on real usage. Every module generates data about how creators actually work. Yggdrasil is refined by real workflows, not guesswork.
  • Creative control, system infrastructure. You make every creative decision. Yggdrasil handles everything around it.
Planned

Ideas fail when they grow so complex that the relationships fracture, the momentum stalls, and the vision drifts.

Yggdrasil exists to prevent that. It is the trunk from which everything branches, and the reason the branches hold.
