Yggdrasil

More than the sum of its parts: a Story Operating System built on layers of idea infrastructure.

  • Index organises your project files and gives AI tools rapid visibility into what exists and where.
  • StoryMap structures narrative logic, character relationships, and thematic coherence across any medium.
  • ProductionMap plans execution: budgets, timelines, logistics, and resource needs mapped to the core project.
  • MarketingMap positions your work: audience targeting, messaging strategy, and channel planning anchored to the creative vision.
  • DomainDataMine advises with real-world intelligence: live industry data fed through an API-driven LLM.
The vision

Ideas fail when they grow so complex that the relationships fracture, the momentum stalls, and the vision drifts. Yggdrasil is the integration layer that prevents that.

It connects each module so context flows freely between them. Not a tool you interact with directly, but the reason every other tool works as part of a coherent whole.
