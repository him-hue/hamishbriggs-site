The vision

Yggdrasil

The trunk from which everything branches: a modular Story Operating System.

World Tree is a Story Operating System built on layers of idea infrastructure. Where existing tools optimize downstream execution, World Tree is designed to harness AI for upstream ideation: the reasoning that makes execution inevitable.

The system works through three layers. Capture: guided intake that structures what the creator provides, so the idea grows tall, not wide. Clarify: canonical understanding that synthesizes all project details into a single "project brain" that can represent the project clearly in any context. Mobilize: domain action that applies that understanding through industry-specific models to navigate real constraints and provide clear next steps.

World Tree does not replace the storyteller. It does not generate the story. It gives humans the infrastructure needed to transform complex projects into coherent, actionable reality, without surrendering authorship or control.

Ideas do not fail because they are small. They fail because they grow so big they can no longer sustain their own weight. The relationships fracture, the momentum stalls, the vision drifts. Yggdrasil, the World Tree, exists to change that.

Each product (Index, StoryMap, ProductionMap, MarketingMap, DomainDataMine) is a module of this system. Together, they form a coherent whole: the idea infrastructure that helps creators maintain coherence and momentum as complexity grows.
