World Tree

Yggdrasil

More than the sum of its parts.

  • Index provides the foundation. StoryMap gives structure. ProductionMap builds momentum. MarketingMap creates reach. All shaped by targeted insight from DomainDataMine. Together, they are more than the sum of their parts. They are Yggdrasil, your World Tree.
  • Unified context. Yggdrasil connects every module so that a change in narrative structure automatically informs production planning, marketing positioning, and domain intelligence. No module operates in isolation.
  • Single project view. Instead of five separate tools with five separate understandings, Yggdrasil gives you one coherent picture of your project from first idea through public release.
  • Compounding intelligence. Each module enriches the others. Story decisions sharpen production estimates. Production constraints refine marketing timelines. Domain data validates every assumption. The system gets smarter the more you use it.
  • Human authorship, machine infrastructure. Yggdrasil does not generate the story, plan the shoot, or write the press release. It gives humans the infrastructure to do all of that with coherence, speed, and confidence.
The vision

Ideas fail when they grow so complex that the relationships fracture, the momentum stalls, and the vision drifts.

Yggdrasil exists to prevent that. It is the trunk from which everything branches, and the reason the branches hold.
