DomainDataMine

Real-world industry intelligence on demand. Sourced, current, and specific to your creative field.

  • Continuous data harvesting. DomainDataMine scours industry databases regularly: box office reports, tax incentives, union rates, distribution windows, platform requirements, and more.
  • Ask a question, get a sourced answer. "What are the SAG-AFTRA rates for a short film shooting in New Mexico with a budget under $500k?" DomainDataMine gives you a real answer grounded in current data, not a guess.
  • Works independently or in context. Use DomainDataMine on its own as a standalone research tool, or within the World Tree ecosystem where it feeds real-world intelligence directly into your production and marketing decisions.
Planned

Every creative industry has hidden rules. Distribution windows, query letter conventions, platform certification requirements. The information exists but it's scattered, paywalled, or buried in forums.

DomainDataMine is being built to consolidate it. Planned for development as part of the World Tree ecosystem.
