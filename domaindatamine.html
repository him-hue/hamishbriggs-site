DomainDataMine

Domain research and synthesis: deep knowledge extraction for storytellers.

DomainDataMine provides specialized domain-specific guidance by applying expert reasoning to structured project data. These modules act as advisory systems, helping creators interpret constraints, opportunities, and next steps within specific industries or domains.

DomainDataMine is currently in the planning phase. Its modules are extensible and can be added across industries as needed: film, television, marketing, publishing, legal, psychology, and beyond.

Become a Design Partner

Every industry has its own constraints, opportunities, and unwritten rules. A filmmaker needs to understand distribution windows. A novelist navigating publishing needs to understand query letter conventions. A game developer needs to understand platform certification requirements. DomainDataMine brings that domain intelligence directly into the project.

Domain modules read the structured project understanding created through Index and the Map layers, then generate advisory guidance tailored to the project's goals and stage of development. External data sources and APIs may be incorporated to provide current, real-world context. Creators retain decision-making authority while using domain expertise to inform choices.
