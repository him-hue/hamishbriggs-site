DomainDataMine

Real-world industry intelligence: domain-specific guidance powered by live data.

DomainDataMine regularly scours industry databases (box office reports, film commission tax incentives, union rates, distribution data, and more) and feeds that information to an API-driven LLM that can advise you, with current real-world context, on what to do with your project next.

It operates as a standalone advisory tool. Over time, it will incorporate derived summaries from other World Tree products, but for now it stands on its own: ask it a question, get an answer grounded in real data.

Every industry has unwritten rules. A filmmaker needs to understand distribution windows; a novelist needs to know query letter conventions; a game developer needs platform certification requirements. DomainDataMine brings that domain intelligence directly to you.

Modules are extensible across industries: film, television, marketing, publishing, legal, and beyond. Full integration with the World Tree ecosystem comes later through Yggdrasil.
